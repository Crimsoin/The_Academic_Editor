Frequently Asked Questions

What types of documents do you edit?

We edit research papers, journal articles, dissertations, theses, grant proposals, and more. If you have a specific document, feel free to contact us for confirmation.

How long does the editing process take?

Standard turnaround is 3-5 business days, but expedited options are available. Turnaround time depends on word count and service type.

Are your editors subject-matter experts?

Yes, our editors have advanced degrees and experience in a wide range of academic fields, ensuring your work is reviewed by a relevant expert.

Is my document confidential?

Absolutely. We treat all documents with strict confidentiality and never share your work with third parties.

How do I pay for your services?

We accept major credit cards and PayPal. Payment instructions will be provided after your quote is confirmed.

Can I request revisions after editing?

Yes, one round of revisions is included with most services. Additional revisions can be arranged if needed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit and debit cards through Payoneer, as well as bank transfers. Please note that all transfer and processing fees must be shouldered by the buyer. Detailed payment instructions will be provided once your quote is confirmed.

Are your editors subject-matter experts?

Yes. Our editors hold advanced degrees and have experience across a wide range of academic disciplines, ensuring your work is reviewed by someone with relevant expertise. All documents also undergo a final check by a senior reviewer with a doctorate degree to ensure the highest quality.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept LaTeX (.tex), Microsoft Word (.doc/.docx), and PDF (.pdf) files.
